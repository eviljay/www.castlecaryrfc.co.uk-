The ground is located off the A371, approximately two miles from Castle Cary town centre in the direction of Bristol/Bath.

 

If travelling from Wincanton/Castle Cary town centre on A371:

  • Drive past the railway station on the right-hand side and Moff Motors on the left.

    •  
  • Turn immediately left just before the Brook House Inn (signposted for Castle Cary RFC)

    •  
  • The Clubhouse is approximately 100m on the right-hand side

 

If travelling from Shepton Mallet on A371:

  • Turn right before or after the Brook House Inn, before you reach Moff Motors
     
  • The Clubhouse is approximately 100m on the right-hand side
