Bristol Rugby Trip

02/05/2006		 < News Homepage
 

On 30th April 2006, over 200 development squad members, parents and coaches attended the end of season outing to Bristol Rugby. The day was a great success

We left the Club at 8.30am in a coach convoy and arrived at Bristol Rugby training ground, where their coaches took training sessions.

After a picnic lunch, we headed for the Memorial Ground and watched the match: Bristol v Northampton. After Bristol led for most of the game, they lost in the last few minutes.

Back to the coaches, having created gridlock to the Bristol traffic! We arrived back at the club around 6.30pm for a well-earned drink!

Many thanks to Karen Travers for arranging a brilliant day. We're looking forward to next year already!
