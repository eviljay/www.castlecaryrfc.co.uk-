Castle Cary Youth Section was re-launched in January 2004.

To date we have approximately 130 members: ages range from 4 to under 17. We meet every Sunday morning: sessions are 10:30 am – 12:00 pm.

Would you like to help?

Volunteers, who give their time for the benefit of members, wholly run the Youth Section; additional help is always needed. A thriving Youth Section is vital to the future of the club, as well as giving considerable value to the youth of our community.

No matter what your talent, there are many ways in which you can help — whether on or off the pitch. E.g., refereeing, coaching, training, helping in the kitchen, administration, merchandiser, etc.

Why not give it a try?

If you are interested, please contact us. Please also take some time to read the guidelines. You can find individual contact details for our Youth coaches on this page.
