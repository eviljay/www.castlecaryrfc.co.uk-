Castle Cary RFC is one of the oldest rugby clubs in the country: in 2010 it celebrates its 122nd year. (You can read the history here.)

We have worked hard over the years to develop a friendly, relaxed club, open to all members of the family and are proud to say that through our efforts we own our Clubhouse and ground outright — something many clubs cannot say!

Our refurbished Clubhouse can be hired for social functions: please click on the link above for details.

All are welcome anytime, with social membership starting from as little as £15 per year. We have a thriving Youth Section and meet on Sunday mornings from 10:30 until mid-day. There is free tea or coffee for parents with bacon or sausage sandwiches for the players.

If you have a Facebook profile, the Club has two group pages:
Castle Cary RFC
